ESP 8266 IOT Sensor

A new variant of the Arduino battery powered node is the IOT sensor which is based on a Wifi node such as the ESP8266.

8266 famility

There are several types of ESP 8266 boards available. They differ in the amount of IO channels/pins available to the use. I do have the ESP-01 version which is the simplest and cheapest form with only 2 IO ports. As a result, pin out is different as well and often these more powerful ESP boards to not have 2.54mm spacing which makes connecting these boards more difficult.
