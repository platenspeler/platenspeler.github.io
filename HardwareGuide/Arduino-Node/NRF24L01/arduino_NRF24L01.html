Arduino Node

The page describes a variant on the Arduino gateway, possibly with another frequency than the standard 433MHz, supporting alternative network topologies such as reliable messaging and mesh network.

 

 

NRF24L01 node

The NFR24L01 is a very, very cheap 2.4GHz transceiver. As it works on 3.3V and uses hardly any power in standby mode, the small device comes in handy when making your own reliable sensor network.
